AntiHarrasment Policy

Why do we have an official Anti-Harresment policy for Developers Students Club NSEC events?

  • Its sets expectations for behaviour at the event. Simply having an Anti-Harresment policy can prevent Harresment
  • It encourages people to attend who have had bad experiences at the other event
  • It gives event staff/ volunteers instructions how to handle harresment quickly, with minimum amount of disruption for the event.

Why do we have an official Anti-Harresment policy for Developers Students Club NSEC events?

  • Gender
  • Sexual Orientation
  • Disability
  • Gender Identity
  • Age
  • Race
  • Religion
  • Nationality

The above is not an exhaustive list -- We do not tolerate harrasment of event spanarticipants in any form.

Sexual language and imagery is not appropriate for any event venue, including talks. Event participants violating these rules may be expelled from the event, and event banned from future at the discretion of the event organizers/management.

Harrasment includes ( but is not limited to ):

  • Offensive verbal comments related to gender, sexual, orientation, disability, gender identity, age, race, religion
  • The use of display of sexual images in public spaces
  • Deliberate intimidation
  • Stalking
  • Harrasing photography or recording
  • Sustained disruption of talks or other events
  • Inappropriate physical contact
  • Unwelcome sexual attention
